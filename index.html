About SANGI Foundation

Mission

To bring people together in acts of compassion by providing sustainable solutions for education, health, environment, and social equity.

Vision

A world where humanity thrives through kindness, cooperation, and inclusive development.

Core Identity

SANGI Foundation is a community-driven NGO promoting empathy, equality, and empowerment. We aim to create lasting impact through collaborative action and sustainable initiatives that uplift communities and protect our planet.

Our Focus Areas

Education & Skill Development

  • Free tuition centers
  • Skill workshops (digital literacy, art, language, music, development classes)
  • Mentorship programs

Health & Wellness

  • Free health check-up camps
  • Blood donation drives
  • Mental health awareness sessions

Environment & Sustainability

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Plastic-free campaigns
  • Clean water/air awareness projects
  • Recycling Art Workshops

Social Care & Humanitarian Relief

  • Food and clothing donation drives
  • Shelter assistance during disasters
  • Elder care initiatives
  • Animal care
  • "Meals of Kindness" weekly food drives
  • Winter blanket & coat drives
  • Nutri-kits for children & pregnant women

Child & Women Welfare

  • "Safe Haven" shelter program
  • Menstrual hygiene distribution / sanitary pad distribution
  • Counseling and protection workshops
  • Maternal health camps
  • Nutrition support for mothers and children
